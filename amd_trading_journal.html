Your complete AMD strategy reference

Daily routine

Step 0 Check Forex Factory for the session date. Any red news 6:00–10:30am PT → skip the day entirely.
6:00am Mark previous day high & low (solid lines). Draw yellow vertical line at midnight. Assess context from midnight to 6:30am.
6:30am Draw opening range box (dotted lines). Must be flat and sideways. Sloping = trending day, skip.
7:00am Watch for manipulation. Do NOT enter yet. Wait for sweep AND reversal confirmation.
Entry First candle BODY closing back inside the range after the sweep. Wick counts for manipulation, body required for entry.

Direction rule

Manipulation sweeps DOWN → trade UP (buy)

Manipulation sweeps UP → trade DOWN (sell)

Context rules

Bullish context — prefer long trades, be cautious on shorts

Bearish context — prefer short trades, be cautious on longs

Neutral context — ALL checklist items must be perfect, aim for 3:1 R:R

Exception: bearish context + bullish signal sweeping the previous day low simultaneously = valid long

Skip signals — no trade if any apply

Red news event on Forex Factory

Opening range candles already sloping

Manipulation slow and grinding, not sharp

No snapback after sweep in 2–3 candles

Major level blocking path to target

Target not reachable within 2–4 hours

Sweep and context point same direction

R:R less than 2:1 (3:1 in neutral context)

Price already trending strongly all session

The golden rules

1. A missed trade costs nothing. A bad trade costs real money.

2. All 5 checklist items must pass — no exceptions, no "close enough".

3. Set stop and target before entering. Every single time.

4. Once in a trade — only exit at stop or target. Not because it feels uncomfortable.

5. Export your journal backup after every session.
